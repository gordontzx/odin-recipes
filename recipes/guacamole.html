Guacamole

home

Ingredients:

  • 3 ripe avocados, peeled and pitted
  • 1/2 small white or red onion, finely diced
  • 2 Roma tomatoes, seeded and diced (optional, to avoid watery guac)
  • 3 tablespoons finely chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1 jalapeño pepper, seeds and ribs removed and finely diced (optional, for heat)
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced (optional)
  • Juice of 1 fresh lime (about 1-2 tablespoons)
  • 1/2 teaspoon sea salt, plus more to taste

Instructions

  • Mash the avocado: In a medium bowl, add the avocado flesh. Use a fork or a potato masher to mash the avocados to your desired consistency. It should be a mix of creamy and chunky.
  • Add other ingredient: Stir in the finely diced onion, tomatoes, cilantro, jalapeño pepper, and minced garlic (if using).
  • Season: Squeeze the fresh lime juice over the mixture and add the salt. The acid from the lime juice helps prevent browning.
  • Combine and taste: Gently mix all ingredients until just combined, being careful not to overmix. Taste and adjust seasonings as necessary, adding more salt or lime juice if needed.
